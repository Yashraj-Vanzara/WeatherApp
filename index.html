Weatherly is a modern and intuitive weather application that provides accurate, real-time weather updates for cities around the world. With a sleek interface and easy-to-use features, users can quickly check current weather conditions, forecasts, and temperature trends. Weatherly is designed to help everyone—from casual users to outdoor enthusiasts—plan their day with confidence, no matter the weather.
